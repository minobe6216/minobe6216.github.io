Japanese Restaurants

Shin Chan Ramen

My favorite dishes from this Nagoya originated ramen restaurant is the Tonkotsu Ramen and the Miso Ramen. Tonkotsu means pork bone and miso is fermented soy bean paste. Through out Japan thereare countless amounts of types of miso. The miso used in this ramen is Nagoya's regional red miso. As an appetizer my reccomendations are Gyoza and Kara Age.

Daruma Restaurant

This is where to go when you want to eat one of the best japanese food not just in Schaumburg but in the world. Their dishes are all very sophisticated and you will be getting what you pay for. My favorite dish so far out of their range of superb dishes is the miso tontoro.
